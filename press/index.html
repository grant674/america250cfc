About the program

The America250 Community Futures Challenge is a national innovation platform built around America's 250th anniversary. The flagship is an open national competition that will award $250,000 across five $50,000 grants to U.S. innovators, entrepreneurs, and community groups whose work materially strengthens an American community.

Submissions open June 2026 and close November 2026. Finalists are notified January 2027. Winners are announced and recognized at a dedicated event in Phoenix, AZ in February 2027.

The Challenge is funded by PMI U.S., designed in partnership with Arizona State University, and operated by Pacheco Ventures. The website at america250cfc.org and the applicant data collected through it are owned and managed by Pacheco Ventures. Submissions are independently judged by a panel of nationally acclaimed leaders.
