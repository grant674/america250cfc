Tell us about your project.

A nationwide open call funded by PMI U.S., designed in partnership with Arizona State University, and operated by Pacheco Ventures. Five winners. $50,000 each. No equity. No strings. Submissions are independently judged across five dimensions by a panel of nationally acclaimed leaders. Your application is collected and managed by Pacheco Ventures and is not shared with PMI U.S. Plan on 25–35 minutes — your progress saves automatically as you go.

Before you begin — how your information is used

The information you provide in this application is collected and managed by Pacheco Ventures to administer the America250 Community Futures Challenge. To learn what we collect and how it is used, see our Privacy Policy and the PMI Corporate Privacy Notice.

Eligibility

A quick check before we get into the details. All five must be yes for your project to be eligible. If anything's not, common eligibility requirements live in our FAQ.

The lead applicant is 21 or older.
The project serves adults 21 and older in a defined U.S. community.
The project is in early-stage, pilot, or scaling phase — not fully self-sustaining.
This is not lobbying, partisan political activity, or individual cash assistance.
No member of our team is an employee or immediate family of PMI U.S., Arizona State University, the ASU Foundation, Pacheco Ventures, or any other named program partners.
