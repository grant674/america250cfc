Who operates the site

The website at america250cfc.org and the application portal are owned and operated by Pacheco Ventures. The Community Futures Challenge is funded by PMI U.S. and designed in partnership with Arizona State University. For legal correspondence, contact legal@america250cfc.org; for privacy and data requests, privacy@america250cfc.org. A mailing address is available on request.

Office hours

The team holds applicant office hours on Tuesdays from 1:00 to 2:00 PM ET via video call between June 2026 and January 2027. Drop a note to apply@america250cfc.org with your name, organization, and a one-sentence summary of your project to receive the link.
